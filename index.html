What do we intend to do?

We seek to improve and maintain optimal mental well-being of the society

Why is it important?

1 in 7 people are suffering from mental illness. However only 40% seek help

How do we plan on doing it?

Mental health education & awareness, workshops & training, medical support

Our Story

All of us have mental health just like our physical health and yet it remains a taboo subject that is susciptible to age-old stigmas, prejudices and fears.

This results in some people suffering from unidentified prolong mental illness. Hence, we wish to educate and spread awareness on the importance of mental health which would further encourage people to seek help at the right time.

Programs for the educational sector

Relive-India's school/college programmes focus on early recognition, mental health promotion and raising awareness about available treatement methods.

Programs for the corporate/ industrial sector

Relive-India's corporate programmes have been designed to focus on spreading awareness regarding mental illness and their treatement.

Programs for specific communities

Relive-India's community programmes aim to address different aspects of mental health among all sects of people and it is adaptable to cater to any particular community
