Programs for the educational sector

Schools and colleges are one of the main transition periods in a child/adolescent's life. it's where we humans learn to establish and maintain social connections outside the family setting, where we learn to regulate our behaviour to an alternate social setting(discipline), among other things. These learnings are vital in achieving milestones for healthy development. However there can be few challenges that children/adolescents may face during their time in an educational institution that may either manifest as a medical illness or further exacerbate an exisiting mental health condition.Re-Live India's school/college programmes and activities focus on early recognition, mental health promotion and raising awareness about available treatement methods.

Our unique school programme model also includes educating teachers and parents in identification and intervention methods. This will aid in early detection and better management of mental health.

Programmes for the industrial sector

According to the ASSOCHAM(2015) recent study, the rate of emotional problems such as anxiety and depression has increased by 45-50% among corporate employees in the last 8 years.

Hence in an effort to address these concerns, our corporate programmes have been designed to focus on spreading awareness regarding mental illness and their treatement.

Programmes for specific communities

Different communities that are part of the larger society need to be aware of basic mental health concerns. Educating them and providing them with the right tools and necessary support can enhance the over-all mental well-being of the society.

Hence our aim is to address different aspects of mental health and it's concerns among all sects of people.

Relive-India's community programmes are adaptable to cater any particular community.
